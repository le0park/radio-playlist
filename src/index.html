        How to use

        Get Music Data From Music Table

        Open MBC Radio website, and enter a music table post that you want to add.

        Then, press below Parse button to get music table data.

        Add Musics to Youtube Playlist

        If you want to add the musics to your playlist, you have to input your playlist ID.

        You can find your playlist ID in URL of your playlist, it's right after list=

        For example, if your playlist url is https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLma835fHabcdefg1RrWN3donthackmezmntM, PLma835fHabcdefg1RrWN3donthackmezmntM is your playlist ID.

        You have to put your playlist ID on below input.

        Then, press below Add to playlist button to get music table data.

        You can see the below list, that has the musics that add successfully or unsuccessfully.
